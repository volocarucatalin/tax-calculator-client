Welcome to the Tax Calculator Client

As a subcontractor, you can use this platform to stay informed about your jobs, payments, and tax deductions. Follow the steps below to understand how this tool helps you track your financials.

1. Create Your Invoice

Start by creating your invoice:

  • Navigate to the Invoice section in the application.
  • You will see a list of invoices including:
    • Job Name
    • Number of Days Worked
    • Payment Rate per day.

2. Check Invoices

Each job has an invoice associated with it. You can view the details of these invoices, including:

  • Total Payment: Calculated based on the days worked and the daily payment rate.
  • Tax Deduction (20%): The tax amount deducted from your earnings.
  • Net Receivable Amount: The amount you will receive after tax deductions.

3. Monitor Payments

The system tracks the status of your payments. You can see whether an invoice is:

  • Paid: The payment has been completed and transferred to you.
  • Unpaid: The payment is still pending.

If you see discrepancies, contact your contractor directly for resolution.

4. Understand Tax Deductions

The platform calculates taxes based on your earnings for each job. Here’s how it works:

  • Total Payment = Days Worked × Payment Rate
  • Tax = 20% of Total Payment
  • Net Receivable = Total Payment - Tax

Example:

You work on Job A for 5 days with a daily payment rate of $200. Here are the calculations:

  • Total Payment: 5 × $200 = $1000
  • Tax Deduction (20%): $1000 × 0.2 = $200
  • Net Receivable: $1000 - $200 = $800

You will receive $800 once payment is marked as Paid by the contractor.

5. View Tax Reports

You can view detailed tax reports that include:

  • Total taxes deducted across all jobs.
  • Net earnings after tax deductions.
  • Detailed breakdowns for each invoice.

6. Keep Your Profile Updated

Ensure your details, such as your Unique Tax Reference (UTR) and contact information, are updated in the system. This ensures smooth operations and accurate tax calculations.

FAQs

  • How is the tax calculated? The system automatically applies a flat 20% tax deduction on your total payment.
  • What if there’s an issue with my payment? Contact your contractor for resolution or further updates.

Start Managing Your Work Today!

Use the Tax Calculator Client to stay informed about your financials, taxes, and payments. It’s simple, efficient, and tailored for subcontractors' needs.
